# IBM HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** using the IBM HR Employee Attrition dataset. The dashboard provides insights into employee demographics, attrition patterns, salary distribution, and job satisfaction to support data-driven HR decision-making.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition across departments
* Explore workforce demographics
* Understand the relationship between work-life balance and employee turnover
* Compare salaries across job roles
* Monitor employee satisfaction trends
* Build an interactive dashboard for HR stakeholders

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* SQL
* IBM HR Employee Attrition Dataset
* DAX Measures

---

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

* Total Employees
* Average Employee Age
* Attrition Rate
* Average Monthly Salary

### Visualizations

* Employee Attrition by Department
* Attrition Distribution by Work-Life Balance
* Gender Distribution of Employees
* Average Monthly Income by Job Role
* Job Satisfaction Trend by Years at Company

### Interactive Filters

* Department
* Gender
* Marital Status
* Business Travel
* Attrition Status

---

## Key Insights

* The overall employee attrition rate is **16.12%**.
* The average employee age is **37 years**.
* Research & Development has the largest workforce.
* Employees with lower work-life balance scores show higher attrition levels.
* Salary varies significantly across different job roles.
* Job satisfaction changes throughout an employee's tenure and may influence retention.
